Simple Career Any% guide

The perfect route to start your speedrunning journey.

Lapglitchâ€‹

Start the run by lapglitching circuits C14, C1, C2 & C3. You can press LB/L1 to lapglitch C14 faster, depending if you're using an XInput or a DSX controller. Then, RB/R1 or LB/L1 twice to lapglitch C1, C2 & C3.

Prologueâ€‹

Watch the absolute cutscene banger and when you gain control of the BMW, use full NOS and go as fast as possible until Mia calls you regarding a mysterious oil leak on your ride.

Complete Ronnie and Bull sprints. Then, you will face Roger (aka Rog) with 2 unique opponents. This race is fully scripted and the opponents, besides their names, will always have the same cars. Usually, after this race you have a cutscene involving Razor and you but you can skip this cutscene very easily. The moment you cross the finish line, restart the race then open the menu, quit back to main menu and go into career again. This glitch will skip the cutscene and will send you directly to Razor sprint.

During Razor sprint, at around 73% of the track, Mia calls you to warn you about an oil leak you left at the starting line. Your BMW has been sabotaged and you will lose the race, resulting in the loss of your car. Then, after the cutscene you will be asked to choose a new car. Choose the Golf (4th car). You will then spawn outside and Mia will ask you to go to your Safe house.

Remarksâ€‹

Rog circuit race is "unlapglitchable" since it's not available in Quick Race. Plus, it has a unique hex address.

After buying the Golf, while driving to the Safe house, turn right where the path splits despite the arrow telling you to go left.

Sonnyâ€‹

Do Race 1 from the Safe house. After finishing the race, turn around and go to the performance shop. Install any spoiler you want and Race Engine.
Do Race 2. Once at the stadium, at around 51% of the race, press start and tune Aerodynamics to +5. After the race, start a chase manually from Bounty 2.

Beginners Sonny chase example

Do the required speedtrap Camera 1 manually from the Milestone menu. (130 km/h | 80 mph)
Do Race 3 then challenge Sonny. Complete both Boss races.
Don't boss skip ! Just spam your Confirm button (A or X). You can use the mouse too. Choose 2 random markers and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Tazâ€‹

At this point, there are 2 possible scenarios here :

  • Scenario 1 â€” You got the cash marker (with or without Sonny's pinkslip / car).

    Upgrade Race Transmission, Pro Suspension and Pro NOS.
    Do Camera 1 (150 km/h | 93 mph).

  • Scenario 2 â€” You got Sonny's pinkslip / car only.

    Go back to the Safe house and sell Sonny's car.
    Lapglitch circuits C4 & C5.
    Quit to free roam, go to the performance shop to your right and upgrade Race Transmission, Pro Suspension and Pro NOS.
    Do Camera 1 (150 km/h | 93 mph).

The following route is the same for each scenario.â€‹

Start Race 4. During this race, after the 2 small S corners, tune Handling to -1, Ride Height to +5 and NOS to +2.
Do Race 3 from the Safe house. If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Then, continue forwards to the stadium and kill everybody chasing you with the stadium pursuit breaker. Escape on the same hiding spot as on Sonny.
If you don't get cops during Race 3, do a manual chase from Bounty 2 instead and do the following chase. You would also do Camera 2 (145 km/h | 90 mph) during this chase.

Beginners Taz "cure" chase example

Go back to the Safe house and lapglitch circuits C4 & C5.
Do Race 1 and Race 6 then challenge Taz. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Izzyâ€‹

Upgrade Pro Tires and Junkman Suspension.
Do Camera 2 (195 km/h | 121 mph) from the Milestone menu. Do Race 7 from the Safe house.
If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Do a minimum of 4 minutes chase and tag 8 cops.
If you don't trigger the cops during the race, do a manual chase from Bounty 2. To spawn the cops faster, do the cooldown trick as shown on the Sonny chase section but do it 3 times instead of twice. Try to trigger a backup as soon as possible.
The bounty requirement for this chase is 18k minimum.
After the chase, go back to the Safe house and lapglitch circuit C6.
Do Race 1. After the finishline, turn left, drive around the donut, turn left a second time and do Vic Camera 2 (170 km/h | 105 mph).
Start Vic Race 1 from the Race menu. Once it loads, quit to free roam, turn around and go for Camera 1 (185 km/h | 115 mph).
Do Vic Camera 1 (175 km/h | 108 mph) from the Milestone menu.
Do Race 5 and Race 6.
Do a Quick Escape (as known as QE) from Bounty 1.

Quick Escape example

Lapglitch circuit C7 and challenge Izzy. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Baronâ€‹

Upgrade Pro Engine, Super Pro Suspension and Super Pro NOS.
Do Race 7 and Race 5.
Do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Do the cooldown trick one time. The chase length has to be 5 minutes minimum. Earn 10k bounty and 10k cost to state to complete 3 milestones.
The bounty requirement for this chase depends on the amount of roadblocks you will get.

After the chase, go back to the Safe house and lapglitch knockout track KO6.
Do Race 1. Do Race 2. After the finishline, continue forwards the downhill, turn left and follow the beach road towards the Amusement Park to do Camera 3 (225 km/h | 140 mph).
Do Race 8 then challenge Baron. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Jewelsâ€‹

Don't upgrade here.
Do Camera 2 (238 km/h | 148 mph) from the Milestone menu.
Do Race 3. After the finishline, continue forwards and hit Earl Camera 2 (220 km/h | 137 mph).
Do Race 4 from the Safe house.
If you trigger the cops in the race, finish the race without losing them. Do a minimum of 6 minutes chase, tag 18 cops and commit 5 infractions to complete 3 milestones.
If you don't trigger the cops during the race, do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Do the cooldown trick one time. Try to trigger a backup as soon as possible.
The bounty requirement for this chase is 368k.
After the chase, go back to the Safe house and lapglitch knockout track KO8.
Do Race 1, Race 2 and Race 6 then challenge Jewels. Complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose the Performance marker, 1 random marker and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Mingâ€‹

Upgrade Super Pro Engine, Super Pro Transmission, Ultimate Tires and Junkman Transmission.
Do Race 10. Some Tier 2 - 3 cars can spawn during this drag such as Carrera GT, Evo IX and a bunch of Audis. It can be hard to win this race due to high rubberband from those cars.
Do Race 7. After the finishline, continue forwards and hit Camera 1 (180 km/h | 112 mph).
Do Race 8.
Do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Do the cooldown trick one time. The chase length has to be 7 minutes minimum, tag 22 cops and avoid 4 spikes to complete 3 milestones. Regarding the 10 roadblocks milestone, it is not necessary to do but can be extra bounty for the next chapter.
The bounty requirement for this chase is not an exact amount. Try to get as much as bounty as you can within the 7 minutes window.
After the chase, go back to the Safe house and lapglitch knockout track KO11.
Do Race 1, Race 4, Race 6 and Race 9.
Before challenging Ming, lapglitch circuit C16 by pressing LB/L1 to reset the tracks region. Challenge Ming and complete both Boss races.
Boss skip ! Choose 2 random markers and confirm to move on to the next boss.

JVâ€‹

Upgrade Ultimate Transmission, Ultimate Suspension and Ultimate NOS.
Do Camera 3 (245 km/h | 152 mph) from the Milestone menu.
Do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Do the cooldown trick one time. Tag 28 cops, commit 6 infractions, earn 325k bounty and 150k cost to state to complete 4 milestones.
Like Ming, the bounty requirement for this chase is not an exact amount. It highly depends on how much bounty you got after Ming chase knowing that you need to accumulate 2300k bounty to challenge him. With this 2300k base, you will need 912k bounty during JV chase, as fast as possible. However, this amount can be drastically reduced if the bounty grinding during Ming chase was good. Bullâ€‹

Upgrade Ultimate Engine and Junkman Engine.
Do Camera 1 (313 km/h | 194 mph) from the Milestone menu. After getting it, immediately take the right way leading to the intersection above, turn right and continue towards Camera 3 (266 km/h | 165 mph).

How to hit both Bull cameras

Go back to the Safe house and lapglitch circuit C23.
Do Race 1. After the finishline, continue forwards and go hit Camera 2 (220 km/h | 137 mph). It is located a bit after the end of the tunnel.
Do Race 2, Race 6 and Race 8. You should get an SMS from Razor putting you immediately on ðŸ”¥ 5.
Do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Don't do any cooldown trick here, the spawn speed of condition 5 cops is already fast enough. Earn 200k cost to state and avoid 8 spikes to complete 2 milestones. It is possible that you get 12 roadblocks before getting 8 spikes, depending on your RNG luck. If so, don't bother avoiding 8 spikes.
The bounty requirement here is 2700k minimum. The more bounty you have at this point will be useful for the next chapter.
Do Race 9, Race 10, Race 11 and Race 12.
Challenge Bull and complete the 3 Boss races.
Don't boss skip ! Choose 2 random markers and confirm to move on to the next boss.

Razorâ€‹

Don't upgrade here since there is nothing to upgrade left.
Do Camera 3 (320 km/h | 198 mph) from the Milestone menu. This camera can be a bit tricky for a new player and might require practice, especially if there is traffic. After getting it, turn left and hit Camera 2 (225 km/h | 139 mph).
Do Race 10 and after the finishline, turn immediately on the left way to hit Ronnie Camera 1 (225 km/h | 140 mph).
Do Race 11 and after the finishline, turn immediately left and go hit Camera 1 (241 km/h | 149 mph).
Do Race 1 and Race 2. Then go back to the Safe house and start Race 3. This race has a particular maxmimum cop probability and should always spawn a cop during it. Nonetheless, you can also finish the race without seeing any cop. If so, do a manual chase from Bounty 2. Earn 850k bounty to complete 1 milestone.
The bounty requirement depends on how much you did get during Bull chase. Like JV chapter, let's take the prerequisite bounty of Bull which is 7550k and draw up a table.

On Race 3, at 60% of the track, there is a hole at the end of the barrier that allows you to sneak through and going Out Of Bounds (as known as OOB). This way you can skip around 1.5km of the track (which represents 24% of the track).

How to perform Razor boss race 3 OOB correctly

Final pursuitâ€‹

Initially, you had to play with cops for 5 minutes before getting called by Mia telling you that you have to go to the old bridge to escape the cops. But you can break this 5 minutes call by performing another OOB in the docks. We call it the "Garage clip".
This OOB occurs in two steps :

  • the "Garage clip",
  • the "Rock jump", this step is a jump where you tilt your car with a certain angle to be able to drive towards the old bridge, avoiding the construction site blocking the original entrance.
